Women Potters of Cyprus

This 26 minute video depicts the work of traditional potters who coil build utilitarian pottery in Cyprus.

All the material was filmed by Dr. London as part of her ethnoarchaeological study
of the potters of Kornos and Ayios Demetrious (Marathasa), in 1987
(funded by a Fulbright Fellowship) and a follow-up one year long study in 1999 - 2000
(funded by a CAARI NEH fellowship).
